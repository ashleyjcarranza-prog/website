Writer • Educator • Creator

---

I’m a writer, educator, and creator based in Las Vegas. I teach both high school and college writing, including courses in the prison system. That work, along with my own life, has shaped how I think about storytelling, voice, and transformation. 

I’m currently working toward my MFA in creative writing and completing my novel A Boy Named Doctor.
